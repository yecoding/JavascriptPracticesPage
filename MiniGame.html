The Pong

Pong is one of the earliest computer games that ever created. It is a table tennis sports game featuring simple two-dimensional graphics. Here I am using p5.js to remake it.

How to Play

The game is for two players. The left player will use keyboard W and S to control the paddle, while the right player will use the P and L keys. The ball will start flying from the center.
